# [[untold]](https://user012100.github.io/functions/)

[untold] is a dedicated space for your creative writing, whether it's poetry, lyrics or anything in between.

## how it works

- type something into the box at the bottom and
- press the arrow to save your piece
- tap any piece to read it
- you can make changes, save it or delete it
- your writing is saved locally automatically to your browser using `localStorage`
